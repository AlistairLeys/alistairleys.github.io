🏥 Healthcare Analytics: Diabetes Risk Prediction System

Business Challenge

Healthcare providers needed systematic tools to identify high-risk diabetes patients for early intervention. With 830 million people globally affected by diabetes (WHO, 2024) and US costs alone reaching $413 billion annually (ADA, 2023), effective risk stratification tools are critical.

Data Science Solution

Developed comprehensive ML pipeline analyzing 442 patient records with Random Forest regression achieving 56% predictive accuracy (p < 0.001). Implemented patient risk stratification with feature importance analysis and clinical validation.

Projected Business Impact

Demonstrated predictive capability with statistical significance • Framework for care pathway optimization • Scalable architecture ready for healthcare system validation

Note: Impact projections are illustrative based on intervention literature and require validation with implementation studies.

Technical Excellence: Python, scikit-learn, statistical validation, cross-validation, clustering analysis, clinical data standards
