Business Problem

Cyclistic, a Chicago bike-share company, seeks to maximize annual memberships by understanding how casual riders and annual members use bikes differently. The company believes converting casual riders represents the greatest opportunity for future growth, as these users are already familiar with the service.

Key Business Questions

  • How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?
  • What usage patterns suggest conversion opportunities?
  • How can temporal trends inform targeted marketing campaigns?
  • What customer segments exist for personalized messaging?

3. Temporal Pattern Analysis: Weekend Concentration

Critical insight: 36.3% of casual trips occur on weekends vs only 24.2% for members - a 12.1 percentage point gap.

# Analyze weekend vs weekday usage patterns df['is_weekend'] = df['day_of_week'].isin(['Saturday', 'Sunday']) weekend_stats = df.groupby('member_casual')['is_weekend'].agg([ lambda x: (x.sum() / len(x) * 100) ]) print("\nWeekend Usage Concentration:") print(weekend_stats)
Weekend Usage Concentration: Weekend % member_casual casual 36.3% member 24.2% Difference: 12.1 percentage points Marketing Implication: Weekend-focused campaigns for casual conversion
